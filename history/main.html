HISTORY

In Ampid, San Mateo, Rizal, a tiny, locally owned company called Reign Rice Retail Store was founded in 2013. Along with other necessary home goods, the business is committed to offering the community a range of premium rice products.


The Reign Rice Retail Store is ideally located to meet the needs of the neighborhood at 19th Road Greenland, San Mateo, Rizal. Residents of Ampid, San Mateo, Rizal, are the store's main target market, particularly those looking for dependable and reasonably priced sources of essentials like cooking oil, rice, and eggs.
